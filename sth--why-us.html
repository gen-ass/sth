With a proud history of over 40 years and through collaborations with prominent alumni and industry partners, the School of Tourism and Hospitality (STH) at UJ continues to produce future-fit leaders for the tourism and hospitality sector.

10 REASONS TO STUDY AT | STH

1# The STH in the Faculty of Management at UJ is an established, recognised and credible institution.

2# The STH offers recognised, credible qualifications at competitive tuition costs.

3# STH programmes are at a higher NQF level. An institution that forms part of a university, offering a university qualification.

4# Undergraduate and postgraduate programmes are offered.

5# The STH offers bursary support

6# Work integrated learning opportunities are available, translated into applied knowledge and technical competencies when entering the workplace.

7# STH combines 40 years of heritage and industry networks that are well established.

8# State of the art training facilities that allow students to gain valuable practical experiences.

9# The STH facility, in addition, offers a platform for students to showcase their culinary expertise through banqueting, conferencing and fine-dining experiences offered at STH venues which are open to the public.

10# National and international partnerships, providing excellent career prospects for students.
